This page is about Moneysign $uede

Jaime Brugada Valdez, also known as Moneysign $uede, is a Rapper known for tracks including "Back To The Bag," "No Cap Freestyle" and "Veteran." He spent time in prison. He earned more than 190,000 followers on his moneysignsuede Instagram account. Before the fame, Instead of going to high school, he got a job working construction at age 14. He signed a deal with Atlantic Records. He collaborated with Bravo The Bagchaser. In June of 2012, he issued a statement after a shooting took place at his show. He grew up with 5 sisters and was compared to kodak black, another known rapper, and they called him Kodak Brown
