Bravo The Bagchaser

This page is about Bravo The Bagchaser

Benjamin Rodriguez, also known as "Bravo the Bagchaser" was born on april 20, 2001, in the united states. Bravo has one son. Rapper and hip-hop recording artist who dropped the singles "Bravo Flexin'," "Bag Right," and "Fool Wit It" in 2019. His popularity led to him amassing more than 280,000 followers on Instagram. His song "Know Better" has amassed over 11 million streams on Spotify. He boasted more than 60,000 monthly listeners on Spotify in early 2020. He is under the label Break In Records. In 2021, he dropped a video for the song "Stay Dangerous", which he collaborated with Blueface and Drakeo, and on YouTube the music video has reached over 4 million views. He collaborated with Fenix Flexin on "Bravo Flexin'."
